AAP Punjab 2022 assembly election manifesto, the ten-point "Punjab Model" of Kejriwal's guarantees (news-grade summary; no full manifesto text was publicly available, the party campaigned on a guarantee card). Key guarantees: 300 units of free electricity to every household and 24-hour power supply. Rs 1000 per month to every woman above 18 years of age. Increased old-age, widow and disabled pensions. Employment and jobs for the youth, ghar ghar rozgar. Free quality education and world-class government schools on the Delhi model. Mohalla clinics and free healthcare in every area. Minimum support price and support for farmers. An anti-corruption helpline to catch and punish bribe-takers, making Punjab corruption-free. Ghar wapasi and return of Punjabi Canadians and NRIs. A Delhi-style governance model of welfare delivery, free services, subsidies and guarantees. AAP won 92 of 117 seats and Bhagwant Mann became chief minister.
