NEW DELHI: Aam Aadmi Party (AAP) supremo Arvind Kejriwal on Monday launched the party s manifesto for the 2025 Delhi assembly elections featuring 15 key Kejriwal ki guarantee. Kejriwal was accompanied by Delhi chief minister Atishi and party leaders Sanjay Singh and Manish Sisodia. Speaking to reporters, the former Delhi CM criticised BJP, accusing the party of mimicking AAP s promise-based governance, stating that We coined the term guarantee first in the country. Employment and women’s welfare: Kejriwal guarantees job opportunities for the youth and announces the 'Mahila Samman Yojna' to support women if AAP comes to power. Free healthcare for elderly: AAP vows to provide free medical treatment for senior citizens. Monthly allowance for women: AAP promises a Rs 2,100 monthly allowance for women, describing it as a "Kejriwal ki guarantee" Job Creation: Kejriwal's first guarantee is "robust" job creation for Delhi residents. Water Bill Waiver: The fourth guarantee promises to waive all outstanding "hiked" water bills. Clean Drinking Water: Round-the-clock supply of clean drinking water to every household in Delhi. Cleaner Yamuna and Better Roads: Commitment to cleaning the Yamuna River and upgrading Delhi's roads to world-class standards within the next five years. Ambedkar Scholarship Scheme: Scholarships under the Babasaheb Ambedkar Scholarship Yojana for SC and ST students to study abroad. Financial Aid to Priests and Granthis: Rs 18,000 monthly financial support to priests and Gurudwara Granthis. Benefits for Tenants: Extension of free electricity and water benefits to tenants and promises to improve Delhi’s sewage infrastructure.
